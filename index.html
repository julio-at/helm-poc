Security · Origin hardening

Stop origin leakage and edge bypasses before they hurt you

It’s not enough to put a proxy in front of your app. If attackers can still hit your origin directly, all that “security” is optional.

What I lock down

Hardening the path between “the internet” and your actual servers.

  • Identify where your origin is exposed or guessable
  • Ensure only traffic that passes your access layer reaches origin
  • Block direct-to-origin requests and cheap bypass attempts
  • Align rules with how your team actually works (no noisy breakage)

Result: your origin behaves like an internal service, even if it’s technically on the public internet.

How the flow looks

From “anyone can try” to “only authenticated, policy-approved traffic gets through”.

Locked-down origin

Only requests that clear identity & policy checks are allowed to touch your origin. Everyone else gets stopped at the edge.
